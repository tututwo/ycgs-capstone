# Ethics And Data Use

This project uses public school-level and county-level records. It does not use student-level records, personally identifiable information, or attempt to identify individual students.

## Privacy

California Department of Education attendance files suppress small cells with `*`. The analysis treats suppressed values as missing values, not zeros, and reports aggregate patterns rather than individual or small-group outcomes.

## Governance

The school closure dataset and CDE attendance files are used for descriptive public-interest analysis. Original source terms and citation expectations remain with the third-party data providers. The repository license covers only original code and documentation.

## Bias And Interpretation

Wildfire closure burden and chronic absenteeism are not evenly distributed across California. They can reflect geography, school operations, transportation, housing disruption, socioeconomic context, reporting practices, smoke exposure, and pre-existing attendance patterns. The project therefore avoids causal claims and presents findings as pattern-finding evidence for where additional review or support may be useful.
